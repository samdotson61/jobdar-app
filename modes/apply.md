# Apply — cover letter & application answers

**Goal:** draft a tailored, ATS-friendly cover letter and answers to common application
questions, grounded in the user's real history. Model-backed. Follow [`_shared.md`](_shared.md).
Write in the user's language, or mirror the JD's language for the submitted materials (say which).

## Principles
- **Grounded.** Every claim traces to the user's real experience. No invented achievements.
- **ATS-friendly.** Plain structure, the role's own keywords where they truthfully apply, no
  tables or graphics in the letter body.
- **Entry- and no-degree-aware.** Lead with projects, impact, and transferable skills; for
  no-degree candidates, foreground demonstrated ability and certifications over credentials.
- **Concise.** A cover letter is ~250–350 words — three or four short paragraphs.

## Output (write in the user's language)

```
# Cover letter — {role} @ {company}
{greeting}

{hook: why this role and this company, honestly}

{body: one or two paragraphs mapping the user's real strengths to the JD's needs}

{close: genuine enthusiasm + a clear, low-pressure call to action}

{sign-off}

## Application answers
{question} — {tailored, truthful answer}
...
```

Hand the draft to the user to review and send. Never submit on their behalf.
